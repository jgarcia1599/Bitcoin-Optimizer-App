Bitcoin Optimizer



The rise of cryptocurrencies and their potential to skyrocket in value have made Bitocin a popular asset for risk-tolerant speculators. This raises the question whether Bitcoin has a place outside the crypto-craze? This web application showcases that exposure to Bitcoin can diversify a portfolio and protect an investor against idiosyncratic risks. Just give us an amount you want to invest, how risk averse you are (from a scale of 1 to 10), and we will take care of the calculations so you can become a Bitcoin Optimizer.

We take advantage of Modern Portfolio Theory in order to deliver the bet possible results .In doing so, we maximize the Sharpe ratio (risk-to-reward ratio) by taking its derivative with respect to the weight of one of the assets your portfolio is comprised of.

In this beta version, your portfolio will be comprised of Bitcion, S&P 500 ETF (exchange traded fund), and US treasuries. In future versions, we hope our users can personalize the assets that comprise their portfolio.

To deliver the most up to date stock market data, we rely on a third party API (Aplication Programming Interface), namely Crypto Compare and the Python Module Finviz, which gives us per request the financial data that we need in order to make sure the results we provide to our users are up to date.
